5 Most Popular Mobile Games of 2023

We all know and love candy crush, Hill climb racing, and Shadow Fight but those are all old. Let's talk about some of the most popular and loved games of 2023.

These are not listed in order.

1. Call of Duty

Call of Duty mobile is a free-to-play shooting action game. It is the mobile version of the famous Call of Duty series from other devices such as Desktops, Xbox, PlayStation and etcetera.

It consists of a variety of guns and skins, both free and paid.

It also has famous guns from the original series such as the P90 which is called CBR4 in the mobile version.



2. Brawl Stars

Brawl Stars is a multiplayer TPP action game. It has a variety of not characters, but brawlers.

These brawlers have various abilities such as:

  • Gadgets
  • Starpowers
  • Gears


3. Genshin Impact

Genshin Impact is a third person, role playing action game. It has a interesting storyline that is progressed on through quests.

It also has various characters with different abilities and backgrounds.



4. Shadow Fight 4: Arena

Shadow fight is one of the most addicting series of fighting games. Shadow fight 4 is one of the most skill and knowledge required games.

It comprises of a set of unique movements and attacks like we've never seen before. It's cut-scene animation is one of the best ever seen in mobile gaming.

The main story line is following a ominous entity called "Shadow Mind" who gains unimaginable power and is unbeatable by anyone

Shadow Mind then asks you to defeat him.



5. Free Fire

Free Fire is a battle-royale, multiplayer shooting game. It became the most downloaded game in 2019 globally

The main hype about this game is about it's skins. In my opinion, it has thebest skins in all of mobile games.
